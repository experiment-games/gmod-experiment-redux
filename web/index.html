Experiment (Redux) is an open-source FPS RPG gamemode for Garry's Mod.

As a test subject in Experiment, you are released into a forsaken city to survive and thrive. You will scavenge for items, complete missions, fight monsters, and compete against other test subjects. During all this a Nemesis AI (originally designed to keep you in check) will try to pit you against other test subjects. This struggle will last an Epoch.

The results of any Epoch can be found in the Heroes of the Epoch leaderboards.

CURRENTLY IN DEVELOPMENT

We've already made a lot of progress on the gamemode. During testing our great testers have provided us with much great feedback.

The greatest result of this feedback is that we've decided to make the gamemode function as a standalone source engine modification. The gamemode will remain open-source, but for the time being we will keep the source engine modification closed-source. This will simplify our development process.

This all means Experiment will be available for more people to play and enjoy.

We have a lot more work to do. It might take at least until Q4 2024 before we can do another public test. Join our to stay up to date with the latest news and updates.
